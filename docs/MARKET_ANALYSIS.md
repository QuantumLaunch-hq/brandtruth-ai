## 2. Target Market Analysis

### Primary Segment: SMB Advertisers

**Definition:** Companies spending $3,000-$50,000/month on Meta ads

**Size Estimate:**
- ~5 million businesses run Meta ads globally
- ~500,000 in our target spend range
- ~50,000 with pain points we solve (TAM)
- ~5,000 reachable in Year 1 (SAM)
- ~500 convertible in Year 1 (SOM)

**Characteristics:**
| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Company size | 10-500 employees |
| Marketing team | 1-5 people |
| Ad management | In-house or small agency |
| Pain level | Medium-high (DIY but struggling) |
| Budget authority | Usually marketing lead |

**Key Pain Points:**
1. Creative fatigue (ads stop working after 2-4 weeks)
2. No time for constant refresh
3. Don't trust generic AI outputs
4. Can't afford agency ($5k-50k/month)
5. Want performance but also brand safety

---

### Secondary Segment: Technical Founders

**Definition:** Startup founders running their own ads

**Characteristics:**
- Technical background (can evaluate AI)
- Small budgets ($1k-10k/month)
- Very time-constrained
- Willing to try new tools
- Value efficiency over polish

**Pain Points:**
1. Don't know how to create ads
2. "Blank page" problem
3. No design skills
4. No marketing background
5. Need results fast

## 8. Risk Assessment

### Market Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| AI commoditization | High | Medium | Differentiate on trust/proof |
| Platform changes (Meta) | Medium | High | Multi-platform, abstraction |
| Economic downturn | Medium | Medium | Focus on SMB efficiency |

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| AI quality issues | Medium | High | HITL, constraints, iteration |
| API rate limits | Low | Medium | Caching, optimization |

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
